About the Liberation War Archive

The Liberation War Archive is a digital platform dedicated to preserving the documents, photographs, and testimonies from the Bangladesh Liberation War of 1971.

Our mission is to ensure that future generations understand the sacrifices and courage of those who fought for independence.
